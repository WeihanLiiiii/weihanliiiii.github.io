I hold a PhD in Finance from the University of Otago, New Zealand, supervised by Prof. Jin E. Zhang, A/Prof. Xinfeng Ruan, and Dr. Pakorn Aschakulporn. My research applies financial econometrics to derivatives markets, with a focus on option-implied risk measures, tail risk, and volatility modelling.

I have published in three ABDC A-ranked finance journals and presented at the New Zealand Finance Meeting and the Derivative Markets Conference. Before my doctoral studies, I gained internship exposure to M&A advisory and audit work, and I hold a Master of Finance (Distinction) from Durham University.
